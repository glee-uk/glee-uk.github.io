FSC Mixtapes

Welcome to our Mixtapes.

These are curated recordings which we’ve sound edited and volume adjusted.

You can stream them from the links below, but we’d really suggest listening to them on an audiobook player. - you can download them to your phone and play wherever you like: cooking at stores, driving to camp, escort coach, you name it.

In these apps the separate songs appear in a “chapterised” list meaning you can see what’s coming next and skip between songs.

They are also CarPlay and Bluetooth compatible.

Some iPhone Chrome users have reported issues downloading: it may be simplest to download on a laptop and WhatsApp to your phone. Or check ‘files’ on your phone in case the mixtapes have downloaded to there instead. If you have issues, drop us an email on ejekerr@gmail.com and jonboden@me.com and we’ll help.
